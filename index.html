THE CHARACTERS
Maria Clara
  • Daughter of Kapitan Tiago and Pia Almba
  • symbol of purity, modesty, virtue
  • often associated with traditional Filipino values and the idealized Filipina image
  • Studied in a convent (Colegio de Santa Catalina de Sena)
  • Fiancé of Crisostomo Ibarra
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Son of Don Rafael , a wealthy Filipino businessman
  • returns to the Philippines from Europe after several years of studying abroad
  • a symbol of hope and reform
  • Fiancé of Maria Clara
Kapitan Tiago
  • Legal Father of Maria Clara
  • A wealthy man
  • "Sacristan Tiago"
  • Husband of Pia Alba
Tiya Isabel
  • Became the "mother figure" to Maria Clara
  • Cousin of Kapitan Tiago
  • Typical rich Filipina during the time of the Spaniards
Donya Pia Alba
  • Mother of Maria Clara
  • Did not have children for a long time
  • Died after the birth of her child
  • Same features of Maria Clara
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • a wealthy Filipino businessman
  • Father of Crisostomo
  • Accused of murder
  • Didn't recieve the justice he deserved
Donya Victorina
  • wealthy Filipina who aspires to be perceived as a Spanish mestiza
  • married to the pharmacist Tiburcio de Espadaña, whom she considers socially inferior and often ridicules.
  • Appears to be more "Spanish" than Filipino
Padre Damaso
  • Former Friar of San Diego
  • Godfather of Maria Clara
  • "Murderer" of Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Spanish
Padre Salvi
  • Replaced Padre Damaso
  • Abuses Sacristans
  • Has an obssession over Maria Clara
  • Spanish
Padre Sibyla
  • Corrupt Friar
  • Had a fight with Padre Damaso
  • Thinks of the church as a "coorporation"
  • Spanish
Tenyente Guevarra
  • Talked to Crisostomo Ibarra
  • warned Crisostomo Ibarra of the Friars
  • knew the reason behind Don Rafael's death
  • Spanish
Alferez
  • The great enemy of Padre Damaso
  • Abusive to his wife
  • Spanish
Pilosopo Tasio
  • Real Name: Don Anastasio
  • Due to his intelligence, his mother stopped him from studying
  • depicted as "Baliw"
  • Has a soft spot for children
Sisa
  • Mother of 2 children, has an abusive husband
  • Thinks highly of her family
  • not wealthy
  • depicted as "Baliw"
Basilio
  • Oldest child of Sisa
  • Became a sacristan due to they don't have enough money
  • Was abused in the church
Crispin
  • Youngest child of Sisa
  • Became a sacristan due to they don't have enough money
  • Was abused and eventually killed
